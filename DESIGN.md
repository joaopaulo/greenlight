# Design System Theme

This document outlines the core visual characteristics of our design system, reflecting the brand's aesthetic and functional requirements.

## Color Palette

Our color palette is built around a vibrant and consistent set of hues, ensuring accessibility and brand recognition.

- **Primary Color**: `#ff7b00` - A bold orange, signifying our brand's energy and primary calls to action.
- **Secondary Color**: `#ac6537` - A warm, earthy brown, offering a supportive role for less prominent elements.
- **Tertiary Color**: `#00a9fd` - A bright blue accent, used for highlights and specific interactive elements.
- **Neutral Color**: `#867369` - A versatile greige, forming the base for backgrounds and foundational UI elements.

The overall color mode is **light**, providing a bright and airy user experience.

## Typography

Our typography system is designed for readability and hierarchy, utilizing a consistent font family across various text types.

- **Headline Font**: `Inter` - For all headings, ensuring strong visual presence.
- **Body Font**: `Inter` - For all paragraph text, optimized for long-form reading.
- **Label Font**: `Inter` - For labels, buttons, and small text, providing clarity and consistency.

## Shape and Form

The geometric properties of our UI elements contribute to the overall feel of the product.

- **Roundedness**: `2` (Moderate) - Corners are moderately rounded, offering a friendly yet structured appearance.

## Spacing

The spatial relationships between elements are carefully considered to create a balanced and intuitive layout.

- **Spacing**: `2` (Normal) - A standard level of whitespace is applied, providing a comfortable visual density without feeling cramped or overly sparse.
